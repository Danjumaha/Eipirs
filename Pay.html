Accepted Payment Methods

For official disbursement processing and fiscal compliance

Important Notice: All transactions are processed under U.S. federal guidelines. This program is not affiliated with any Nigerian entity, scheme, or individual. Any claim suggesting "Nigeria involvement" is false and misleading.

PayPal

Secure digital payments via verified PayPal accounts. Funds are traceable and compliant with U.S. financial regulations.

Wire Transfer

International or domestic bank wire transfers. Requires SWIFT/BIC and full banking details for verification.

Cash Deposit

In-person cash deposits at authorized U.S. Treasury partner banks. A valid government-issued ID is required.

Cryptocurrency

Accepted in Bitcoin (BTC) or Ethereum (ETH) only. Transactions must be reported for IRS compliance.

Money Order

U.S. Postal Service or bank-issued money orders payable to "U.S. Department of the Treasury".

Cashier's Check

Official bank-issued check with guaranteed funds. Must be drawn from a U.S.-based financial institution.

All payment methods are subject to verification, audit, and U.S. federal compliance protocols.
